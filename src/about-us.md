## On the legal side

Legally, the OCaml Software Foundation (OCSF) is a sub-foundation of
the [INRIA Foundation](https://www.inria.fr/). It is a charity/non-profit -- read our
[statutes](/assets/pdfs/RI-OCaml.pdf) for more details.
